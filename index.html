key combinations

keys-
  • 'x + mouse clicking': move object to center (DO THIS FIRST!)
  • -
  • 'a': turn left
  • 'd': turn right
  • 'w': turn up
  • 's': turn down
  • -
  • 'q': zoom in
  • 'e': zoom out
